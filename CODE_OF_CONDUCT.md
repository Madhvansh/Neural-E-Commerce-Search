# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good faith while discussing ideas and evidence.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.
- Keep technical criticism focused on the work rather than the person.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, trolling,
and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method on the maintainer's
GitHub profile. The maintainer may edit or remove content and temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical, and retaliation against reporters is prohibited.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
